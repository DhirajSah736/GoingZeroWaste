Dont waste food

Its like wasting one of the most precious things given by God to us. Give away food instead of throwing it away in garbage bags. Today's wastage is tomorrow's shortage. Don't bite off more than you can chew.

Our mission

Our main goal is to save food

Collect surplus food from local food retailers that would otherwise go to waste and redistribute the food to the people who actually need it.

Why you should join us

We need your Help!

Volunteers who are willing to help the hungry and feed them can join us in this selfless service to transfer the uneaten food to the people who actually need it.
